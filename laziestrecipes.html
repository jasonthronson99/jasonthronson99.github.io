Recipe 1: Microwave Mac & Cheese

What You Need

  1. Large Bowl (Either Regular One Or Disposable Large Dixie Bowl)
  2. Fork/Spoon For Eating & Mixing
  3. Some Type Of Colander (Makes The Process A Lot Easier)
  4. Microwave Safe Plate (For Making Bowl Easier To Carry)
  5. One Mac & Cheese Box
  6. 3 Tbsp Any Butter (No Butter Substitutes)
  7. Water
  8. A Few Splashes Of Milk
  9. Shredded Cheese (Colby Jack If Using White Cheddar Box, White Italian Blend If Using Regular Cheddar Box)
  10. Optional: Any Meat (I Like Pouch Meat Like Pouch Chicken For This)

Instructions

  1. Add Macaroni From Kraft/Annes Box To Bowl & Fill About 1/2 Inch Above Pasta With Water
  2. Microwave For 3 Minutes
  3. Take Out Of Microwave & Stir, Then Place Back In Microwave
  4. Microwave For 3 More Minutes
  5. Drain Pasta, Then Add All Other Ingredients & Mix Well
  6. Microwave For 60-90 Seconds

Recipe 2: Simple Yogurt Bowl

What You Need

  1. Large Reusable Bowl
  2. Spoon For Mashing & Eating
  3. 1 Cup Frozen Berry Mix That Doesn't Have Strawberries
  4. Small Amount Of Nut Butter (Like 2 Tbsp)
  5. 1 Scoop Of Your Favorite Protein Powder (I Like Nutricost Chocolate Whey)
  6. Some Plain Yogurt (Either A Lot Or A Little Depending On Your Preference, Personally For Me I Like Using Those Yogurt Cups You Can Buy At The Store)

      7. Instructions

      1. Add berry mix to bowl, and microwave for 45-60 seconds
      2. Use a spoon to mash the berries, then add all the other ingredients and mix very well
